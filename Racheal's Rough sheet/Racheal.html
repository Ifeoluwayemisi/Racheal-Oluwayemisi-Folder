ANEAMIA

Aneamia also called Lack of Blood. it is the condition in which the blood dosen't have enough red blood cell or dysfunctional red blood cells in the body, this leads to reduced oxygen flow to the body organs.





    CAUSES OF ANEAMIA

  • Lack of vitamin b12
  • Lack of folate in our diet
  • Inherited blood disorder(like sickle cell aneamia or Thalassemia)

WHAT IS THALASSEMIA?

thalassemia is a Inherited blood disorder that causes the body to have less hemoglobin than normal.

FUNCTIONS OF RED BLOOD CELLS

The main function of red blood cell is it carry oxygen from our lung to the rest of our bodies, then they make the return trip, taking carbon di oxide (O2) back to our lungs to be exhaled.

HEMOGLOBIN:

hemoglobin enables red blood cells to carry CO2
