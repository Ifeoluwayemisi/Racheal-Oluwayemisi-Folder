INTRODUCTION TO HEADACHE

HEADACHE is any form of pain in any region of the head. Headache may occur in one or both side of the head, be isolated to a certain location , radiate across the head from one point, or have a versatile quality. A Headache may appear as a sharp pain, a throbbing sensation or a dull ache.

THROBBING is a pulsing, beating sensation that happens over and over again.

DEFINITION OF HEADACHE

Headache, also known as CEPHALALGIA, is the symptomsof pain in the face, head,or neck.

It can also be define as a pain in the head with the pain with the pain be above the eyes or the ears, behind the head (occipital), or in the back of the upper neck
